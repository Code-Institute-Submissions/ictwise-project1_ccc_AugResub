About

Caerphilly Cycling Club is a friendly cycling club where the focus is on enjoying your riding. We have members interested in all aspects of cycling. From social riding to sportives to long distance epics. If you are looking for some colleagues to race with, train with or just to get out and ride with then please come along and give us a try.

The club was formed by a group of keen road cyclists who wanted to create a place for cyclists of all abilities to meet up, ride together and have fun doing it. The club has three established rides per week encouraging riders of all abilities and experience, from road cycling enthusiasts to many people who have little experience of riding in a group or have never been part of a club. During the winter months the Sunday ride continues and the mid week rides are put on pause until Spring. There should be something for everyone and new riders are always welcome.
