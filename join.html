Join Our Club

New riders are more than welcome to two trial rides before deciding to join Caerphilly Cycling Club. After that we ask that you join us and pay the appropriate membership fee. The club year is from April 1st until March 31st every year. The cost of club membership is £20 if you join the club before September 30th. After that date we realise that you're not having the benefit of club rides in the evenings. However, there are other activities through the winter so the cost of joining after that time would be £10. If you pay the full membership amount of £20 and join the club in February or March then your membership will be carried over for the next full year.

Strava
